FAQ

Is there a free plan?

Yes. Starter lets you list 1 project with reels, hotspots, and WhatsApp leads at ₹0. Upgrade anytime.

How do I pay?

Use online checkout (card or UPI). You’ll receive GST invoices automatically. You can manage payment methods and cancel anytime via the Billing Portal.

Yes. Plans are month‑to‑month. Annual billing gets 20% off.

Starter is effectively a pilot. If you need more during evaluation, write to support and we’ll enable a timed Growth trial.
